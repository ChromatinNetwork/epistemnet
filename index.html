EpiStemNet

EpiStemNet contains a comprehensive network of epigenomic relationships in mESC. This epigenetic map describes a collection of 139 previously described assays including ChIP-Seq, MeDIP and GLIB analysis. As a consequece, we derive a network of ESC regulators containing 3 types of cytosine modifications (5mC, 5hmC and 5fC), 13 histone marks and 61 Chromatin related Proteins (CrPs) including the ESC master regulators Nanog, Oct4 and Sox2

A large scale chromatin and DNA methylation network in embryonic stem cells. Network construction, functional analysis and co evolutionary model. Enrique Carrillo de Santa Pau, Juliane Perner, David Juan, Simone Marsili, David Ochoa, Ho-Ryun Chung, Daniel Rico, Martin Vingron and Alfonso Valencia

Under review Nature Genetics
